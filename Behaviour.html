Dos and don'ts of a job interview

There are a few things you should do during an interview. Firstly, you should try to drive the discussion, it is important to make interview more of a conversation. You should also try to ask your own questions about the company and job. Another important thing is to find a good way to demonstrate your qualifications. For example, you can demonstrate them through a story or by showing examples of your previous projects. The last thing is smile, that is because smile makes you fell happier and more self-confident. The thing you should avoid during the interview is being too ambitious, you should be realistic.
