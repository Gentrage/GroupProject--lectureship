How to answer typical and tricky interview questions

Question: "What are your biggest weaknesses?"

Advice: You can make your weakness becomes your advantage. You can also say that you are working on to improve your weaknesses.

Question: "What kind of work environment do you like best?"

Advice: You should think about the company you are applying and about the work environment in that company. For example if the company expects employees to self-manage you should say that you like self-manage.

Question: "Tell me how you think other people would describe you."

Advice: You should say you are not sure what other people think about you, but you think that you have a good opinion among other people.

Question: "What is your leadership style?"

Advice: You should give some examples when you were leading the group and dealt with a problem.

Question: "What questions do you have for me?"

Advice: You should ask some smart question to see if the company is good for you.

Question: "What are your biggest strengths?"

Advice: When you talk about your strengths you should give some examples to prove it.

Question: "What can we expect from you in your first three months?"

Advice: You should use general framework. For example you can say that you will work hard and focus on doing what you do best.

Question: "Why do you want this job?"

Advice: You should say why this work is perfect for you and what you want to achieve.

Question: "Why do you want to leave your current job?"

Advice: Don’t talk about the bad things, that happened to you in your job. Talk about things that you learned by working here and your vision for the future.
