How to deal with the recruitment documents

How to deal with cover letter?


You should always include a cover letter, unless the job advertisement clearly says not to. What is a cover letter? It's a single-page letter that you include with your job application. Remember to use a different one for each job you apply for. When you are writing a cover letter, firstly you should introduce yourself and mention about the job or kind of job you're applying for. After that You have to present your skills and experience, You also need to show how they match the needs of the job. One of the most important things is to encourage the reader to read your resume. Finally, finish with a call to action (for example, asking for an interview or a meeting). Keep your cover letter quite short. A cover letter should be a summary of your resume, so don’t write more than one page.

How to deal with CV?


The most important thing about formatting your student resume is dividing it into sections. Your CV should be clear, brief, complete, and up-to-date with your work experience and educational information. The basic components that make up a good CV are: Contact Details, Personal Profile , Skills , Work Section, Education Section and Hobbies Section. The elements that you include and their order will depend on what you are applying for. For example, if you haven’t extensive experience, you should focus on your education, mention your academic achievements and relevant coursework but even then, don’t forget about your work experience section. Include relevant jobs in the work experience section: even part-time jobs or volunteering. The best style of employment record is the chronological curriculum vitae. Remember to put your career history or education in reverse date order starting with the most recent experience. In contact details section, that will be on the top of your CV, you have to include your full name, email address, contact number and home address. In Personal Profile it is important to mention about your features of character and abilities which might be useful in your future job. For example, if you are applying for a shop assistant role, express your passion to help customers or to develop your experience in a retail environment. When you are creating Skills Section, firstly look at job advertisement and write down all the skills they require, highlight most relevant abilities. Hobbies Section should mentioning your interests allows your personality to shine through.
