Personality traits and universal people skills that are the most desired

Skills:

- Time management - it is important to meeting deadlines and staying efficient

- Problem solving - you should be able to find the best way to solve the problem

- Teamwork - even positions with a lot of independent work will still require you to collaborate with others

- Critical thinking - ask intelligent questions and draw conclusions

- Communication - you can solve most problems with the proper communication


Personality Traits:

- Adaptability - you can handle new situations

- Reliable - you keep your word

- Sociable - you like working with other people

- Imaginative - you are able to find new ways to solve the problem

- Helpful - you know how to help your colleagues
